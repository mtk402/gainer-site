Why Ironside exists

Ironside is built in Next.js and wrapped with Capacitor for iOS to deliver a fast, native-feeling app that stays simple, powerful, and privacy-first. Your training history belongs to you: local-first storage, easy import from mainstream apps, and full export when you want it. No lock-in, ever.
